# EchoPact

A synchronous integrity layer for human communication. Where promises go to persist—quietly, constitutionally, without judgment.

**EchoPact** is a cognitive commitment engine inspired by the principles of constitutional safety and attentive memory. It watches your messages for pledges ("I'll send that Monday", "I'll review by Thursday"), remembers them across contexts, and follows up with respectful precision. When you need silence, it goes quiet. When you need accountability, it speaks with clarity.

---

## Overview

Every day, thousands of promises are made in written communication—emails, chat messages, task boards. Most are forgotten. Some are kept. A few are broken, eroding trust. EchoPact is designed to bridge the gap between intent and action, without adding friction or judgment.

Think of it as a **cognitive witness**—an impartial yet caring observer that captures the essence of your commitments and surfaces them at the right time. It doesn't nag. It doesn't shame. It simply remembers so you don't have to.

## Why EchoPact?

Most productivity tools assume you want to be *more productive*. EchoPact assumes you want to be *more trustworthy*. The difference is subtle but profound.

Productivity is about doing more. Trustworthiness is about doing what you said you would do. EchoPact optimizes for the latter, recognizing that a single kept promise is worth a hundred unchecked tasks.

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. **Watch Mode** – EchoPact listens (with permission) to your communication streams.
2. **Pattern Recognition** – Constitutional safety filters detect promise-like language ("I'll", "I promise", "by Friday", "let me check").
3. **Commitment Mapping** – The promise is stored with context: who made it, to whom, when, and what the expected deliverable is.
4. **Silent Verification** – EchoPact waits. No alerts. No reminders.
5. **Graceful Follow-Up** – After a configurable quiet period, EchoPact asks: "Did this happen?" The answer is yours to give—or ignore.
6. **Memory Evolution** – Over time, EchoPact learns your communication patterns and becomes more accurate at detecting real promises vs. casual talk.

## 🛡️ Constitutional Safety Framework

EchoPact operates under a strict constitutional safety layer that ensures:

- No promises are stored without explicit user awareness
- No follow-ups occur during designated quiet hours or focus sessions
- No judgmental language is used in any interaction
- No data is shared with third parties
- No amplification of negative patterns (e.g., reminding you of broken promises in a way that induces shame)

This framework makes EchoPact safe for vulnerable contexts—therapy, recovery, leadership, parenting, and any environment where trust rebuilding is critical.

## 🔒 Privacy & Data Control

Your promises are yours. EchoPact is merely the guardian of memory.

## 🧪 Development Philosophy

EchoPact is built with an ethos of **gentle accountability**. It is not a task manager. It is not a habit tracker. It is a relationship tool—one that honors the fragile nature of human trust.

Every feature is tested against a simple question: *Does this increase or decrease the user's sense of safety?* If it decreases safety, it doesn't ship.
